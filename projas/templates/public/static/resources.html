Links to AI starting points on the web

Below you will find links to some other internet services on the web. Each of these services maintains a large collection of pointers to various AI resources on the Web. Enjoy!

AAAI
An electronic service maintained by the American Association for Artificial Intelligence.
ACM SIGART
An electronic service maintained by the Association of Computing Machinery's Special Interest Group on AI.
AI on the Web
A list of various AI resources on the web, with links, maintained by Stuart Russell.
Yahoo Catalog of AI Resources
Another catalog of AI resources on the net. There is a section on AI Journals.

Links to other AI publications and publishers on the web

AAAI Press
Publisher of the PROJAS bound volumes. AAAI Press also publishes books on AI-related topics.
The Journal of Machine Learning Research
The Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR) provides an international forum for the electronic and paper publication of high-quality scholarly articles in all areas of machine learning. Free on the web!
Electronic Transactions on Artificial Intelligence (ETAI)
A new type of web publication covering AI.
IEEE Expert
Focuses on expert systems. Table of contents and abstracts.
