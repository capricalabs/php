PROJAS(ISSN 11076 - 9757) covers all areas of artificial intelligence (AI), publishing refereed research articles, survey articles, and technical notes. Established in 1993 as one of the first electronic scientific journals, PROJAS is indexed by INSPEC, Science Citation Index, and MathSciNet. PROJAS reviews papers within approximately three months of submission and publishes accepted articles on the internet immediately upon receiving the final versions. PROJAS articles are published for free distribution on the internet by the AI Access Foundation, and for purchase in bound voumes by AAAI Press.
